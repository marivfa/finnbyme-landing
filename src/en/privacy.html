Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Overview

This app is designed to help you understand, organize, and improve control over your personal finances. We prioritize privacy and collect only the data necessary to provide core functionality.

This application is currently in early access.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Account information (email address)
  • Financial data you choose to enter (e.g. expenses, categories, notes)
  • App usage data in aggregate form only (e.g. feature usage counts)

Personal identification details are optional.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not:

  • Access your bank accounts
  • Automatically import transactions
  • Sell or share your data with third parties
  • Review individual user transactions manually

4. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to:

  • Provide financial tracking and insights
  • Generate summaries and educational suggestions
  • Improve app features based on aggregated usage

All financial decisions remain entirely yours.

5. Educational Purpose Disclaimer

This app provides educational insights and suggestions, not financial advice.

Any recommendations or insights are:

  • Informational only
  • Optional to follow
  • Not instructions or guarantees of outcomes

You are solely responsible for any financial decisions you make.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Encrypted data storage
  • Secure authentication via Amazon Cognito

No system is 100% secure, but we strive to protect your information using industry-standard practices.

7. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from within the app. This permanently removes your personal data from our systems.

8. Changes

As this app evolves, this policy may be updated. We will notify users of significant changes.
